Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 7, 2025
The short version: Prewatchr doesn't collect your personal data, doesn't track what you watch, and doesn't require an account. The only thing stored is a summary count — and it lives in your browser, not our servers.

What Prewatchr does

Prewatchr is a browser extension for Chrome and Firefox that generates AI-powered summaries of YouTube videos so you can decide whether to watch them — before you click.

Stored locally on your device

The extension stores one piece of information in your browser's local storage:

  • The number of summaries you've generated

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us.

What happens when you request a summary

When you click a YouTube thumbnail with Prewatchr armed, here's exactly what happens:

  • The video URL is sent to our server
  • The transcript is fetched from YouTube
  • The transcript is passed to an AI model to generate a summary
  • The summary is returned to your browser
  • All data is immediately discarded — nothing is stored on our end

What we do not do

  • Store your browsing history
  • Track which videos you watch
  • Collect personal information
  • Require login or accounts
  • Share data for marketing or advertising
  • Sell your data to third parties

Infrastructure providers

We use third-party services to ensure reliable delivery of transcripts. These services process video URLs temporarily during the summary request. No user data is stored or shared beyond real-time processing.

Data security

  • All communication between the extension and our server uses HTTPS encryption
  • Video data is processed in real-time only — no persistent storage
  • No user accounts or passwords are required or stored
  • Local statistics remain only in your browser

Your control

You have full control over your data:

  • Clear all local data at any time by uninstalling the extension
  • Stop using the service at any time — there's nothing to cancel
  • Request any information we might hold by contacting us below

Children's privacy

Prewatchr is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
