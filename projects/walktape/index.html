what it does

A

Touch-point precision

Put the phone at the point instead of guessing where a screen tap landed.

B

Walk any route

Cable runs, pipe paths, fences, room perimeters — get the true path length, the straight-line distance, and the vertical change.

C

PDF plan reports

A top-down plan view with measurement tables, ready to send to a client.

D

Heights without a ladder

Ceilings, walls, and stairwells — the floor is detected automatically.
