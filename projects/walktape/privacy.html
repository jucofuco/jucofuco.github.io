WalkTape — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

WalkTape is built so that your data stays on your device. This policy explains, in plain language, what the app does and does not do with information.

What we collect

Nothing. WalkTape has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no servers of its own. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

Camera

WalkTape uses your device's camera together with Apple's ARKit to measure distances, paths, and heights. Camera images are processed entirely on your device, in real time, and are never recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

Motion & sensor data

Device motion sensors are used on-device by ARKit to track the phone's position while you measure. This data never leaves your device.

Your measurements

Measurements, session data, and exported files (PDF reports, point-cloud files) are stored locally on your device only. You decide where exports go when you share them using the iOS share sheet. Deleting the app deletes all locally stored measurement data.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment details. Apple's handling of purchase data is described in Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children

WalkTape does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date.
