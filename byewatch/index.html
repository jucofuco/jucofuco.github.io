Privacy Policy for SumThing for YouTube

Last Updated: December 7, 2025

What We Do

SumThing is a browser extension that generates AI summaries of YouTube videos to help you save time.

Information We Collect

Stored Locally (On Your Device Only)

  • Number of summaries you've generated

This data never leaves your browser.

Processed (Not Stored)

When you request a summary:

  • Video URL is sent to our server
  • Video transcript is fetched from YouTube
  • Transcript is processed by AI
  • Summary is displayed to you
  • All data is immediately deleted

We do NOT:

  • Store your browsing history
  • Track which videos you watch
  • Collect personal information
  • Require login or accounts
  • Share data for marketing

Third-Party Services

Google Gemini AI

  • Generates summaries from transcripts
  • Privacy: https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms

YouTube (Google)

  • Source of video transcripts
  • Privacy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Infrastructure Providers

  • We use third-party services to ensure reliable delivery
  • These services process video URLs temporarily
  • No data is stored or shared beyond processing

Data Security

  • All communication uses HTTPS encryption
  • Video data processed in real-time only
  • No user accounts or passwords required
  • Local statistics stored only in your browser

Your Control

You can:

  • Clear all local data by uninstalling the extension
  • Stop using the service anytime
  • Request information: juraj.seba@gmail.com

Children's Privacy

Not intended for children under 13.

Updates

We may update this policy. Check this page periodically.
